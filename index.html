Performance

The Car is built for speed and precision. With a mid-engine layout, high-performance V8 engine, and lightweight chassis, it delivers exhilarating acceleration and track-ready handling.

Aerodynamic Design

Every curve and vent is engineered for maximum aerodynamics. The aggressive stance, low drag coefficient, and functional air vents ensure optimal downforce and cooling, making it a true supercar on the road.

Advanced Technology

Experience the future of driving with a digital cockpit, performance data recorder, and driver-focused controls. Stay connected with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a premium Bose sound system for the ultimate ride.
